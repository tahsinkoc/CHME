# FX Pricing, Fee Transparency, and Margin Controls

## Scope
This document defines operational standards for foreign exchange pricing in the FinTech SaaS platform. The objective is to keep controls deterministic, auditable, and compatible with snapshot based recovery.

## Control Objectives
The control plane for fee and spread governance must enforce rate source validation, fee disclosure, and spread limit enforcement. Every workflow should preserve event ordering, maintain clear owner attribution, and surface decision evidence for audits.

## Signals and Monitoring
Primary monitoring signals include: rate source drift, customer fee complaint, unusual conversion pattern. Secondary indicators include transaction retries, customer communication lag, and unresolved alert age. Daily control reviews must compare expected versus observed activity.

## Failure Modes and Recovery
Common failure modes include stale quote usage, hidden fee variance, margin breach during volatility. Recovery starts with containment, then root cause isolation, then deterministic replay using snapshot aligned state. Every incident closes with a checklist that updates control logic and runbooks.
