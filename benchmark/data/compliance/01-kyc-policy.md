# KYC Policy and Customer Identity Verification

## Scope
This document defines operational standards for know your customer workflow in the FinTech SaaS platform. The objective is to keep controls deterministic, auditable, and compatible with snapshot based recovery.

## Control Objectives
The control plane for identity verification must enforce document validity, beneficial ownership, and customer risk profile updates. Every workflow should preserve event ordering, maintain clear owner attribution, and surface decision evidence for audits.

## Signals and Monitoring
Primary monitoring signals include: customer profile change, sanctions hit, transaction burst. Secondary indicators include transaction retries, customer communication lag, and unresolved alert age. Daily control reviews must compare expected versus observed activity.

## Failure Modes and Recovery
Common failure modes include incomplete onboarding, stale identity documents, unresolved alerts. Recovery starts with containment, then root cause isolation, then deterministic replay using snapshot aligned state. Every incident closes with a checklist that updates control logic and runbooks.
