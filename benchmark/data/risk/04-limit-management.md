# Dynamic Limit Management and Exposure Guardrails

## Scope
This document defines operational standards for limit management lifecycle in the FinTech SaaS platform. The objective is to keep controls deterministic, auditable, and compatible with snapshot based recovery.

## Control Objectives
The control plane for exposure controls must enforce limit tiers, override approval chain, and emergency cap reduction. Every workflow should preserve event ordering, maintain clear owner attribution, and surface decision evidence for audits.

## Signals and Monitoring
Primary monitoring signals include: limit utilization spike, override frequency rise, concentration risk. Secondary indicators include transaction retries, customer communication lag, and unresolved alert age. Daily control reviews must compare expected versus observed activity.

## Failure Modes and Recovery
Common failure modes include limit drift, override abuse, delayed exposure review. Recovery starts with containment, then root cause isolation, then deterministic replay using snapshot aligned state. Every incident closes with a checklist that updates control logic and runbooks.
